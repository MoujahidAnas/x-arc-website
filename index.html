The Solution

A trained intern that evolves with your business.

Track 1

The boring stuff that eats your day. Your intern takes it.

  • Writes in your voice because it learned your tone, your clients, your preferences.
  • Sends the email, posts the content, follows up with the client. Delivered in your channel, not a chat window.
  • Remembers what worked. Applies your corrections everywhere, permanently.

The useful stuff you never get to. Your intern builds it.

  • Ships features, automations, and internal tools to production. Working systems, not code snippets.

Common Questions

What you're probably wondering.

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT answers questions. Your intern does the work. It knows your business, remembers yesterday, and delivers finished output. Not text for you to copy-paste.

Do I still have to manage it?

You give direction and review output. It handles execution. You stay in control of decisions.

What if it doesn't work for my business?

That’s what the discovery call is for. We look at your setup together. If there’s no fit, we’ll tell you.

What tools does it work with?

Your intern lives in your communication channel — Slack, WhatsApp, Telegram, or whatever your team uses. From there, it connects to your email, documents, and project tools. We configure everything during onboarding.

How much does it cost?

Two options. A monthly subscription - a fraction of what you’d pay a human hire - where your intern handles ongoing operations and grows with your business. Or a project engagement, where our internally trained digital interns build and deliver a specific system for you. We’ll figure out the right fit on the discovery call.

How is this different from other AI agents?

Most AI agents are generic bots with access to your system. No training, no verification. Your X-Arc intern is trained on your context, runs every task through a verified pipeline, and operates within controlled boundaries. You don’t configure anything. We deploy, train, and maintain it.
